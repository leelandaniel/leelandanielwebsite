About

Leeland Daniel, born Leeland Daniel Murnane on December 31, 2009, is a singer/songwriter and producer from Indianapolis, Indiana. He began his music career at age 12 under the stage name "GoldenCraftr," releasing his first song "Love You" in 2022. In February 2025, he rebranded to "Leeland Daniel" for a fresh start.

  • More

  • Since starting at just 12 years old, Leeland has released 2 solo singles under the name Leeland Daniel. His stage name, GoldenCraftr was the very beginning of Leeland's music career. He released his first song, Love You, on November 11, 2022, which was co-written by fellow producer, writer, and artist, Stephen Flickner He then went on and released his first EP, "Messing with my Feelings" which consisted of three tracks, Love You, Can't Love You, and Without You. GoldenCraftr has since been retired. Leeland Daniel's first single, "falling" was originally released under his stage name "GoldenCraftr" on February 7th, 2025 But shortly after he changed the artist name.

    In February 2025, Leeland rebranded from "GoldenCraftr" to "Leeland Daniel" he left his stage name in the past because he wanted a fresh start with his music. Leeland stated on his public Snapchat story, "I am no longer GoldenCraftr. I decided to make a fresh start with my music and in the next week or so, my artist profiles will be found under the name Leeland Daniel. The only song that is transferring artist profiles, is my newest one. (falling)".
